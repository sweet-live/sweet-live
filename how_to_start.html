    HOW TO START GAMING

    A lot of people who are just started their path in gaming world have problems with overall understading of what they need to buy to be completely ready for the journey. The answers are here!

    CONSOLE OR DESKTOP PC

    The most important thing before buying any gaming device you need to choose how do you want to play them? If you want to play on the couch infront of TV, sometimes with your friends or family at the same place the best choice for you will be to buy gaming console, because you can easily get an extra remote and play it from the same TV, however other purposes about which we will talk later, won't be available to for the console.

    If you want to have more isolated or personal gaming sapce then PC will be the best choice for you. You will have a personal corner where you can stay just with yourself focusing on games more deeply.

    DECIDE YOUR BUDGET

    Once you decided which type of gaming and games you want to experience you need to also understand how much you want to spend on your gaming experience.

    First of all, console gaming will be way cheaper than a PC gaming. The reason is not the gaming station itself, but the accesories. While to play console you need only TV that majority already have, to play PC you will need to acquire computer mous, keyboard, headphones or speaker, and monitor.

    The second, if you decided to get the PC, because consoles have a limited price ranges comparing with PC's, and you already decided games you want to play you need to check the system requirments of the games you choose. Depends on system requiremnets you will be able to start choosing the parts of your PC. YES. Parts, this is the most interesting topic for majority of the gamers. It is very interesting process of selecting your own set up becasue there is a huge spectrum of PC parts that you can choose. This web page will help you get more detailed information about price ranges and all specifications. If you don't want to build PC yourslef, that is pretty popular way to save some money and make sure that all components are high quality, you can use for understanding which complete build you should buy to not spend extra money on it.

    OTHER PURSPOSES OF PC

    If you have chosen a PC then it's will be very helpful for you, or if you not, you possibly can rethink about it.

    PC is able to run other programs that are pretty useful nowadays. Video editing, photo processing, or 3d design need quite powerful system, so if you decided to get a decent computer with high specifications, you will be able to work there without being worried that your pc will be lagging or will not run it at all.

    There is much much more programs that you will like and again we highly reccomend you to visit Logical Increments if you didn't check before. This is one of the best pages, if not the best, that will provide you a huge amount of information about PC's, so you will become a knowledgeable user and you will choose what you need very quickly.

    PC aesthetics

    Getting a beutiful colorful devices became very popular nowadays. However RGB lightning on the PC parts or devices dosen't mean that they are the best. You need to consider that it is just for aesthetics and beautiful atmosphere.

    However, you will not find a low specifications PC parts with RGB colors, becasue cheaper GPU's (Graphic Processing Unit) or just graphic cards are not considered as gaming GPU's.
